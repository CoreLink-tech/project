FAQ

Most Common Questions

Everything you need to know about trading on Quadzedge.

I can't sign in to my account?+
You're likely entering incorrect authentication data. If you can't remember your password, use the password recovery function. Press "Forgot password?" on the login page and follow the instructions. Check your Spam folder if the email doesn't arrive in your inbox.
What is Copy Stop Loss?+
Copy Stop Loss (CSL) is an instruction to close a copy if the value of the entire copy drops below a specific dollar amount or ratio. You can use this to protect your investment. CSL is mandatory on every copy and can be set anywhere between 5% and 95%.
How do I make a deposit?+
Log in to your account. Click on 'Deposit'. Enter the amount you wish to deposit. Select the payment method from the dropdown menu. If crypto is selected, copy the wallet address and send your funds. Deposits are confirmed within minutes.
What is Copy Trading?+
As the name implies, it's copying trades from an experienced trader. You select a trader, and whatever positions they open, you open automatically. The profits and losses are proportional to your investment amount.
How do I withdraw my profits?+
Very straightforward. Log in to your account and click 'Withdrawal'. Enter the withdrawal amount and your desired method. Select the account to withdraw from and click proceed. Most withdrawals are processed instantly or within 24 hours.
Is it safe to share my information here?+
Our services are completely confidential. We do not share any user data with third parties. All data is encrypted and protected. Please see our privacy policy for complete details.
Do you offer a referral program?+
Yes. We have a referral program with earnings ranging from 10% to 30% depending on your plan tier. Check your user dashboard to access your unique referral link and track your earnings.
